TitledCollectionReusableViewFactory

A TitledCollectionReusableViewFactory is specialized supplementary view factory that conforms to CollectionSupplementaryViewFactoryType. This factory is responsible for producing and configuring TitledCollectionReusableView instances.

The factory has the following type parameters:
